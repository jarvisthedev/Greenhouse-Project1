Medium technology Greenhouse

Is regarded as a moderate cost and technology level used greenhouse. Due to the minimal investment, greenhouse users prefer a manually or semi-automated control system. Galvanized iron (G.I) pipes are used to create this type of greenhouse. With the help of screws, the canopy cover is attached to the framework. To withstand wind disturbance, the entire construction is firmly attached to the ground. The temperature is controlled by exhaust fans with controls. Evaporative cooling pads and misting systems are also used to keep the greenhouse at a comfortable humidity level.he minimal investment, greenhouse users prefer a manually or semi-automated control system.
